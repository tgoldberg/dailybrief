Revenue The total gross sales you processed in a day. It's worth noting that this does not deduct credit card processing fees which would reduce the total amount you receive. This is useful so you know how much money your store made.
Orders The total number of orders you received in a day. Your customers may place multiple orders in a day.
Items The total number of items sold across all orders you recieved in a day.
Top Items The top five items sold (based on quantity) in a day. It also highlights the total gross revenue generated from each item. This is useful so you can see what drove the majority of your sales.
Most used discount codes The top five most used discount codes in a day. It also highlights the total amount deducted across all orders where that discount code was applied. This is useful so you can see which discounts drove the most sales.
